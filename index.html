Who We Are

Atmagaurav Infra Private Limited

Atmagaurav Infra Private Limited is a government infrastructure contractor headquartered in Bhayander East, Thane, Maharashtra. Since our inception in 2024, we have been delivering critical infrastructure projects that connect communities and drive economic growth across India.

We specialize in highway construction, bridge engineering, road maintenance, and road safety systems — all executed to the highest standards set by NHAI, MORTH, IRC, and State PWD.

Our team of experienced engineers, project managers, and skilled professionals brings deep domain expertise and a passion for excellence to every project we undertake.
